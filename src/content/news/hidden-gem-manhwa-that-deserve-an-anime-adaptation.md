# 10 Hidden Gem Manhwa That Deserve an Anime Adaptation in 2026

While titles like **Solo Leveling**, **Tower of God**, and **The God of High School** have introduced millions of viewers to Korean webtoons, countless incredible manhwa are still waiting for their chance to shine on the big screen.

Many of these series feature breathtaking artwork, emotional storytelling, unforgettable protagonists, and world-building that rivals some of today's biggest anime. Yet, despite their popularity among dedicated readers, they remain overlooked by animation studios.

If you're searching for your next binge-worthy read, here are ten hidden gem manhwa that we believe deserve an anime adaptation.

---

## 1. The Boxer

**Genre:** Sports • Psychological • Drama

At first glance, *The Boxer* looks like another sports series, but it quickly becomes much more than that. The story explores talent, trauma, ambition, and what it truly means to be strong.

### Why It Deserves an Anime

- Emotionally powerful storytelling
- Incredible character development
- Unique visual style
- Perfect for a 24-episode adaptation

---

## 2. Leviathan

**Genre:** Action • Survival • Sci-Fi

Humanity now survives on scattered ships after giant sea monsters dominate Earth. Every journey across the ocean becomes a fight for survival.

### Why It Deserves an Anime

- Beautiful monster designs
- High-stakes survival action
- Stunning world-building
- Great potential for cinematic animation

---

## 3. The Ember Knight

**Genre:** Fantasy • Strategy

Unlike typical overpowered protagonists, Nagy survives using intelligence instead of brute strength. His clever tactics and constant outsmarting of stronger opponents make every battle unpredictable.

### Why It Deserves an Anime

- Intelligent protagonist
- Excellent pacing
- Rich fantasy world
- Strong supporting cast

---

## 4. Worthless Regression

**Genre:** Fantasy • Regression

After living a life filled with regret, the protagonist receives another opportunity to change his fate. Instead of becoming instantly overpowered, every improvement feels earned through determination and relentless training.

### Why It Deserves an Anime

- Slow but rewarding character growth
- Beautiful emotional moments
- Fantastic action sequences

---

## 5. The Skeleton Soldier Couldn't Protect the Dungeon

**Genre:** Dark Fantasy • Adventure

A weak skeleton repeatedly dies and returns with memories of previous lives, gradually uncovering the mysteries surrounding his world.

### Why It Deserves an Anime

- Unique time-loop mechanics
- Excellent mystery
- Memorable cast
- Incredible long-term storytelling

---

## 6. Survival Story of a Sword King in a Fantasy World

**Genre:** Fantasy • Action

After spending decades trapped in a tutorial, Hanbin finally enters the real world stronger than anyone could imagine. Despite its humorous beginning, the story evolves into an epic fantasy adventure.

### Why It Deserves an Anime

- Fantastic action choreography
- Great comedy
- Outstanding character development

---

## 7. Her Summon

**Genre:** Fantasy • Adventure

One of the most visually stunning manhwa ever published. Every chapter feels like concept art for a blockbuster fantasy film.

### Why It Deserves an Anime

- Exceptional artwork
- Creative monster designs
- Incredible fantasy world
- Emotional storytelling

---

## 8. Epic of Gilgamesh

**Genre:** Fantasy • Adventure

Set in the same universe as *The Ember Knight*, this series delivers an unforgettable journey filled with mystery, politics, and memorable characters.

### Why It Deserves an Anime

- Rich lore
- Fantastic world-building
- Deep character relationships

---

## 9. Player

**Genre:** Action • Fantasy

After entering the world of his favorite webtoon, the protagonist must survive alongside powerful heroes and terrifying monsters. The story combines fast-paced action with humor and emotional moments.

### Why It Deserves an Anime

- Entertaining cast
- Beautiful battles
- Strong fantasy setting

---

## 10. The Horizon

**Genre:** Psychological • Drama

Unlike most action-focused recommendations, *The Horizon* tells an emotional story about two children trying to survive in a war-torn world. It is heartbreaking, unforgettable, and one of the highest-rated manhwa ever created.

### Why It Deserves an Anime

- Masterpiece-level storytelling
- Powerful emotional impact
- Award-worthy animation potential

## Why Are More Manhwa Getting Anime Adaptations?

The global popularity of Korean webtoons continues to rise each year. Successful adaptations have shown that audiences are eager for fresh stories beyond traditional manga.

With streaming platforms investing more in international content, the chances of underrated manhwa receiving anime adaptations have never been higher.

While there are no official announcements for the titles listed above at the time of writing, each has the storytelling quality, dedicated fanbase, and visual appeal to become a successful anime.
