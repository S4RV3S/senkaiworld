# Jujutsu Kaisen Review

## Overview

Jujutsu Kaisen has become one of the defining anime of the new generation. With stunning animation by MAPPA, memorable characters and fast-paced storytelling, it successfully modernizes classic shonen while maintaining emotional depth.

## Story — 9.4/10

The plot follows Yuji Itadori as he enters the dangerous world of Jujutsu Sorcerers. Every arc introduces stronger curses, shocking twists and meaningful character development.

## Animation — 9.8/10

MAPPA delivers spectacular fight choreography with fluid animation, creative camera work and cinematic visual effects.

## Characters — 9.5/10

Yuji, Gojo, Megumi and Nobara form one of modern anime's strongest main casts, while the villains remain equally memorable.

## Music & Sound — 9.3/10

The soundtrack perfectly complements both emotional scenes and intense battles.

## Pros

- Incredible animation
- Excellent fight choreography
- Strong supporting cast
- Great pacing
- Emotional story

## Cons

- Some arcs move very quickly
- New viewers may find the power system complex

## Final Verdict

Jujutsu Kaisen is everything a modern action anime should be. Stylish, emotional and endlessly entertaining.

**Final Score: 9.5/10**
