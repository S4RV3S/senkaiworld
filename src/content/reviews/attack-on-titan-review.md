# Attack on Titan Review

## Overview

Attack on Titan is more than an action anime—it's a gripping political thriller, mystery and war drama. Its incredible storytelling and unforgettable twists have secured its place among the greatest anime ever made.

## Story — 9.9/10

Every season expands the world in unexpected ways, revealing shocking truths that completely change the narrative.

## Animation — 9.7/10

Both WIT Studio and MAPPA delivered exceptional animation that captures the scale and intensity of every battle.

## Characters — 9.8/10

Eren, Mikasa, Armin, Levi and the supporting cast undergo remarkable development throughout the series.

## Music & Sound — 9.8/10

The soundtrack perfectly captures both emotional moments and large-scale conflicts.

## Pros

- Outstanding storytelling
- Incredible world-building
- Excellent character development
- Memorable soundtrack
- Powerful ending

## Cons

- Slow pacing in some political arcs
- Complex plot requires close attention

## Final Verdict

Attack on Titan is a modern masterpiece that every anime fan should experience at least once.

**Final Score: 9.8/10**
