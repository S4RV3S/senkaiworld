# The Beginning After the End (TBATE) Review & Complete Guide

The Beginning After the End, often shortened to **TBATE**, has become one of the most beloved fantasy manhwa of the past decade. Written by TurtleMe and beautifully illustrated by Fuyuki23, the series combines reincarnation, magic, politics and emotional storytelling into a compelling adventure that appeals to both longtime fantasy readers and newcomers alike.

Unlike many reincarnation stories, TBATE places just as much emphasis on personal growth and relationships as it does on action. Its protagonist, Arthur Leywin, carries the memories of his previous life, but instead of becoming instantly invincible, he must slowly earn his strength while navigating a completely different world.

## Story Overview

Before his reincarnation, Arthur was known as King Grey, a ruler feared for his unmatched strength but haunted by loneliness. Despite possessing everything a king could desire, he lived a life without genuine friendships or family.

After his death, Grey awakens as Arthur Leywin, a child born into a loving family in the magical continent of Dicathen. Remembering every mistake from his previous life, Arthur vows to live differently. Rather than pursuing power alone, he learns to value family, friendship and responsibility while training to master magic at an incredibly young age.

As Arthur grows older, his peaceful childhood gradually gives way to dangerous conflicts involving powerful mages, ancient races and a looming war that threatens the entire continent.

## Main Characters

Arthur Leywin is one of the strongest protagonists in modern fantasy because his greatest battles are often emotional rather than physical. Although gifted with exceptional talent, he constantly questions whether he can truly protect the people he loves.

Sylvie, Arthur's dragon companion, quickly became one of the series' most beloved characters thanks to her loyalty, intelligence and emotional connection with Arthur.

Tessia Eralith plays a major role throughout the story, developing from a sheltered princess into a capable mage with her own ambitions and struggles. Supporting characters such as Virion, Alice, Reynolds and the professors of Xyrus Academy further enrich the world and make every arc feel meaningful.

## World Building

One of TBATE's greatest strengths is its detailed fantasy world. Magic is divided into multiple elements, kingdoms possess unique cultures and political tensions influence nearly every major event.

Rather than introducing new concepts without explanation, the story gradually expands its world through Arthur's journey. Readers learn about mana manipulation, beast wills, ancient civilizations and powerful enemies at a natural pace that keeps the narrative engaging.

## Artwork

The artwork consistently improves throughout the series. Character designs are expressive, magical battles are visually impressive and emotional moments receive just as much attention as action scenes.

The artist successfully captures both the peaceful beauty of Arthur's childhood and the darker atmosphere that emerges as the story progresses.

## Why TBATE Became So Popular

TBATE stands out because it balances exciting action with genuine emotional depth. Arthur's relationships with his family, friends and mentors feel authentic, making readers care about the consequences of every battle.

The pacing also deserves praise. Rather than rushing the protagonist's growth, the series allows Arthur to mature gradually, creating a satisfying sense of progression.

## Final Verdict

The Beginning After the End is one of the finest fantasy manhwa available today. With exceptional world-building, memorable characters, emotional storytelling and exciting action, it delivers an experience that continues to improve with every chapter. Whether you're already a fantasy fan or looking for your first manhwa, TBATE is an outstanding choice.

**Rating: 9.8/10**
