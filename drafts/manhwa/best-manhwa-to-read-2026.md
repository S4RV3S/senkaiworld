# Top 15 Best Manhwa to Read in 2026

If you've been wondering what manhwa deserves a spot on your reading list this year, you're in the right place.

The Korean webtoon industry continues to grow rapidly in 2026, producing stories with stunning artwork, unforgettable characters, and some of the best world-building in modern comics. Whether you're a fan of Murim, fantasy, romance, or overpowered protagonists, these recommendations represent the best the medium has to offer.

---

## 1. Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint

**Genre:** Action • Fantasy • Apocalypse

Kim Dokja is the only reader who completed an obscure web novel. One day, that novel becomes reality, forcing him to survive using knowledge only he possesses.

**Why Read It?**

- Incredible world-building
- Outstanding plot twists
- Amazing character development
- Beautiful artwork

---

## 2. The Greatest Estate Developer

**Genre:** Comedy • Fantasy • Isekai

Lloyd Frontera uses engineering instead of brute force to save his kingdom. Packed with hilarious moments and clever storytelling, it's one of the funniest manhwa available today.

---

## 3. Return of the Mount Hua Sect

**Genre:** Murim • Martial Arts

Legendary swordsman Chung Myung returns one hundred years into the future only to discover that the Mount Hua Sect has fallen into ruin.

His journey to rebuild it is both hilarious and inspiring.

---

## 4. Eleceed

**Genre:** Action • Supernatural

Jiwoo's peaceful life changes forever after meeting Kayden, one of the strongest awakeners trapped inside a cat.

Excellent action combined with comedy makes Eleceed one of the easiest recommendations for new readers.

---

## 5. The Beginning After the End

**Genre:** Fantasy • Reincarnation

After dying as King Grey, Arthur Leywin begins a new life in a magical world full of danger, dragons, and political conflict.

---

## 6. SSS-Class Suicide Hunter

Every death makes the protagonist stronger—but at an emotional cost.

One of the best-written modern action manhwa.

---

## 7. Legend of the Northern Blade

A visually stunning Murim masterpiece featuring breathtaking sword fights and mature storytelling.

---

## 8. Nano Machine

Nanotechnology meets martial arts in one of the most addictive power-progression stories available.

---

## 9. Doom Breaker

Humanity has already lost.

Given one final chance, Zephyr returns to the past to rewrite history.

---

## 10. Pick Me Up: Infinite Gacha

A deadly mobile game becomes reality where every decision determines survival.

---

## 11. Revenge of the Baskerville Bloodhound

Dark fantasy meets revenge in a brutal story filled with political intrigue and unforgettable battles.

---

## 12. Absolute Sword Sense

What if swords could speak?

This unique premise creates one of the freshest Murim experiences in recent years.

---

## 13. Academy's Genius Swordsman

A fast-paced fantasy series filled with academy rivalries, magic, and exceptional swordsmanship.

---

## 14. Myst, Might, Mayhem

One of the breakout Murim hits of 2026 featuring a ruthless protagonist and unpredictable storytelling.

---

## 15. The Extra's Academy Survival Guide

Instead of becoming the hero, the protagonist is reincarnated as a side character who must survive the story's deadly events.
